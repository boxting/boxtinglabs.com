# Boxting Labs Design Identity

## Brand Concept
**Boxting** is about the "Box" — the fundamental unit of structure in software, layouts, and engineering. The brand identity is built on **Neo-Brutalism**, emphasizing raw structural elements, bold strokes, and high-energy motion. It's a "Software Factory" that is both unpolished in its honesty and premium in its execution.

## Visual Language: Neo-Brutalism
- **Boxes:** Everything is contained in boxes with defined, thick borders (2px - 4px).
- **Corners:** Sharp, 0px border-radius to emphasize the "Box" in Boxting.
- **Shadows:** Hard, non-blurred shadows (4px - 8px offset) instead of soft glows.
- **Grid:** Visible grid lines or patterns that evoke a blueprint or a factory floor.
- **Motion:** Kinetic, sudden, and rhythmic. Animations should feel like mechanical parts moving in a factory.

## Typography
- **Headings:** `Syncopate`
  - *Mood:* Kinetic, wide, futuristic.
  - *Usage:* H1, H2, Hero titles.
- **Body:** `Space Mono`
  - *Mood:* Monospaced, technical, precise.
  - *Usage:* Body text, labels, navigation.

## Motion & UX Rules
1. **Loading States:** Use a "Box Assembly" animation where boxes stack or expand to reveal content.
2. **Entrances:** Elements should slide in with a "Spring" effect, as if being snapped into place.
3. **Hover States:** Instead of soft fades, use "Offset" shifts (moving the box or its shadow) and color inversions.
4. **Brutalism around Boxes:** Every section should be clearly defined by a box. Overlapping boxes create depth without using transparency.

## Social Media Identity
- **Visual Motif:** Use the "B" from Boxting enclosed in a heavy-stroke box.
- **Patterns:** Diagonal stripes (Warning tape style) and dot grids.
- **Typography Layout:** Large, bold text that overflows the container, clipped by the box edges.
